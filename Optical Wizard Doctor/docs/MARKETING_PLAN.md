OCR-Orc serves organizations and individuals who need **precise, manual coordinate calibration** for OCR processing of handwritten forms. Our target audience falls into distinct segments:

- **Size**: Small to medium agencies (not enterprise)

- **Volume**: Medium to high (hundreds to thousands of forms)
- **Budget**: $200-$1000 per license
- **Pain Points**:
